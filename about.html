About

Hi, I'm Mud.

Welcome to Mud Laboratory.

I'm an IT professional with a passion for websites, cloud technologies, and programming. Mud Laboratory is my digital workshop—a place where I document what I build, what I learn, and the experiments that help me grow as a developer.

My goal is simple:

To become the kind of developer businesses can trust to design, build, deploy, and maintain modern websites. I may not know everything yet, but I'm committed to learning every day, improving with every project, and sharing that journey along the way. I'm currently following a structured learning journey to strengthen my web development skills, one project at a time.

What I am learning:

I am learning how to build a website confidently on my own and develop it further. I am also learning how websites work, from designing the user interface to deploying them on the internet.

My Vision:

By continuously learning and building real projects, I aim to become a developer who can confidently design, build, deploy, and maintain modern websites for businesses of all sizes.
